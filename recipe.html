Pizza with salami, olives & fresh greens


Ingredients:

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 6 tbsp tomato passata
  • 6-8 sliced salami
  • 100 g cheddar cheese
  • 70 g olives
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • sliced rocket leaves
  • 70 g cherry tomatoes

Spread the base of pizza with passata, cheddar and salami. Bake it for 8-10 min. Grind over rocket leaves and salsa from olives and chopped cherry tomatoes. Serve hot.

Pizza with prosciutto & spicy salsa


Ingredients:

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 6 tbsp tomato passata
  • 10 slices prosciutto
  • 100 Emmental cheese
  • 70 g olives
  • extra virgin spicy olive oil
  • mix of leaves
  • 70 g cherry tomatoes, halved

Spread the base of pizza with passata, cheese and prosciutto. Bake it for 8-10 min. Grind over mix of leaves and spicy salsa from olives and chopped cherry tomatoes. Scatter with basil.

Chicken pizza with mushrooms

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 6 tbsp tomato passata
  • 200 g chicken breast
  • mix of cheddar and mozarella
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 100 g white mushrooms
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Fry white mushrooms and chicken breast until golden. Spread the passata, cheese mixture, chicken, and mushrooms over the pizza's base. Bake it in the oven for about 8-12 minutes until crunchy. Pour over more olive oil and oregano. Serve hot. Optional to add fresh leaves.

Salmon pizza with crunchy crust

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 6 tbsp tomato passata
  • 200 g lightly salted salmon
  • 1/2 ball mozarealla
  • 70 g olives
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • green onion
  • 70 g cherry tomatoes, halved

Fill the base of pizza with passata and mozarella. Bake it for 8-10 min. Grind over salted salmon and salsa from olives, green onion and cherry tomatoes. Drizzle with extra olive oil. Scatter with basil and present hot.

Chorizo pizza with mozarella

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 6 tbsp tomato passata
  • 6 slices chorizo
  • 10-20 small ball mozarella
  • 6 roasted red peppers, torn
  • 70 g olives
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • green onion
  • 70 g cherry tomatoes, halved

Add mozzarella, chorizo, and passata to the pizza's foundation. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes, or until crisp and golden. Grind the olives, green onions, and cherry tomatoes into a salsa. Put in a little more olive oil. Add black pepper and basil, then serve hot.

Grilled chicken pizza with white sauce

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 10 slices of barbeque grilled chicken breast
  • 10 pcs potato fries
  • 100 g olives nad cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 ball mozarella

Combine 70 g of sour cream, 1/2 tsp mustard, half a lemon, chopped parsley, and a small amount of heated water to make the white sauce. Add sauce, mozzarella, and chicken to the pizza's base. Bake for 8-10 min. Put olives and cherry tomatoes on the top.

Pancetta pizza with potatoes

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 6 tbsp tomato passata
  • 2 tbsp pancetta, cooked
  • 1 tsp thyme leaves
  • 4 potatoes, cooked and thickly sliced
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 ball mozarella
  • 70 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 100 g mushrooms, cooked

Add tomato passata to the pizza's bottom layer, then arrange the potatoes and mushrooms on top, garnished with thyme. Drizzle with the pancetta and arrange the mozzarella pieces over top. Bake it for 8-10 minutes until golden and crisp. Accompany with olives and cherry tomatoes.

Pepperoni Pizza with mushrooms

  • 500 g pizza dough
  • 6 tbsp tomato passata
  • 6 slices pepperoni or chorizo
  • 6 roasted red peppers, torn
  • 70 black olives
  • 1/2 ball mozarella
  • 100 g white mushrooms
  • 70 g cherry tomatoes

Spread the base of pizza with with passata, mozarealla, fresh mushrooms, peppers and pepperoni. Bake it for 10-15 min. Grind over olives and chopped cherry tomatoes.
