Hello everyone!

Welcome to the culinary haven of Andrei and Yuliia, an international couple from Romania and Ukraine whose love is rivaled by passion for crafting perfect homemade pizzas. Join us on a journey that began right after our wedding ceremony, as we discovered art of pizza making together. Yuliia has mastered delicate dance of dough preparation, achieving a perfect balance of softness and crispiness. Andrei, a self-taught chef, curates the finest ingredients, from the best Italian prosciutto to rustic salami from Ukraine, Poland, and Romania. Our kitchen is a canvas where cultural influences blend into flavorful symphonies. Explore our website for expert advice, mouthwatering recipes, and a glimpse into the rich tapestry of European flavors.
